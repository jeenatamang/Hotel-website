Hotel Country Villa

About us

Nestled at the peaceful foot of the hills in the scenic village of Shyafru, Rasuwa, Hotel Country Villa offers the perfect blend of comfort, tranquility, and natural beauty. Whether you're a trekker heading towards Langtang, a nature lover seeking serenity, or a traveler exploring the Himalayas, our hotel is your ideal home away from home.
Our charming property features 20 well-furnished rooms, each with an attached bathroom for your privacy and convenience. We offer a variety of accommodations to suit your needs, including double suites, triple bedrooms, and cozy single-doubles, all designed with your comfort in mind.
Wake up to the fresh mountain air, enjoy warm hospitality from our friendly staff, and take in the breathtaking views right from your window. At Hotel Country Villa, we’re dedicated to making your stay restful, memorable, and uniquely local.
Come stay with us and experience the heart of Rasuwa – peaceful, picturesque, and perfectly you.
